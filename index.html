Talbotiella gentii Planes

Had the previlage to work with some great lectures on a research project, mapping the Talbotiella gentii planes. The Talbotiella gentii is enlisted as a "Critically Endangered" species on the IUCN Red list. This is one of the few areas in Ghana that still have these species. The entire field data was collected with the Mavic 2 Pro and Phantom 4 Pro for aerial photos supported with Emlid Reach RS2+ GPS for coordinating GCPs

Data for the map: OpenStreetMap
Tools for map: QGIS, Canva

Krachi East Roads

This is one of the maps created for the Department of Urban Roads (DUR) - Ghana, as part on a project to digitise and document all existing roads in Ghana.
This project involved the creation of a geodata base with the recommended fields as demanded by DUR. All foundational data were obtained form OpenStreetMap and cleaned (updated with additions and substractions), then field checked to confirm data.
Data source: OpenStreetMap
Tools: QGIS, ArcGIS Pro, PostgreSQL

Unsupervised Classification of Asuogyaman

As part of my MPhil program, I enrolled in the course "Advanced Remote Sensing." For the course project, each student was required to select a district (a subdivision of a region in Ghana) and obtain satellite imagery for three different years, with a five-year interval between each dataset. The task involved applying various clustering methods for unsupervised classification and analyzing the changes over time.
Data source: USGS (Landsat 8)
Tools: QGIS
